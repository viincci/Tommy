Bio

Tommy, who was born in Johannesburg in 1963, spent 1968â€“1979 studying at the Kutlwanong school for the deaf. While still in school, he started drawing, and after graduating from high school, he pursued his art studies at the Johannesburg Art Foundation and FUBA. He started exhibiting after receiving a grant from Sanlam in 1985. One of his paintings was displayed at a global exhibition in Monte Carlo in 1986, and he was awarded a distinction medal in the Volkskas Atelier exhibition in 1987. He was chosen for the Cape Town Triennale shortly after that. Additionally, he received the Standard Bank Young Artist of the Year honor. Collectors admire his art, and significant public and private collections can be found all around South Africa.
